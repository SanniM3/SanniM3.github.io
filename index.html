About me

I'm an AI Researcher and Engineer specialising in natural language processing, speech recognition, and large language models. My work spans building models from scratch, fine-tuning them for specialised domains, and designing evaluation methods that probe their various capabilities. I've contributed to published research at top venues and developed AI solutions that are in production. My goal is to carry this same blend of innovation and execution into my next role — advancing AI that is both powerful and responsible.

I enjoy turning research into real-world systems — whether that's improving ASR performance for underrepresented accents, creating high-quality datasets, or building agentic AI systems that plan, reason, and take action across complex workflows. I approach problems with both scientific rigour and an engineering mindset, ensuring solutions are robust, scalable, and human-centred.
